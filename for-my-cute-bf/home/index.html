To My Beloved

In the quiet moments when I see you, I find a universe of comfort and understanding. Your presence in my life has painted colors where there was once only gray.

Every laugh we share becomes a melody I carry with me. Every tear we've wiped away has strengthened the bond between us.

With you, I've learned to see beauty in the smallest things - the way morning light filters through curtains, the sound of rain against windows, the silence we can share without words.

I cherish not just who you are, but who I become when I'm with you - braver, kinder, more complete.

This journey with you is the greatest adventure of my life, and I look forward to every step we take together. Happy Birthday!

Forever yours, Skye
